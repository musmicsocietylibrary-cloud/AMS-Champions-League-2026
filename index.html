About

AMS Champions League Tournament 2026 is a multi-sport event for Societies from Academy of Medical Sciences (AMS) school; 24–26 April 2026. It brings students together to compete in different games, build unity, teamwork, and friendship, and promote discipline, sportsmanship, and excellence through the competition.
